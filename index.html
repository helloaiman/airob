Tips For Better Tracking
💡 Lighting — face a light source; backlighting confuses detection.
📏 Distance — keep your hand roughly 30–60 cm from the camera.
🖐️ Framing — keep your whole hand inside the video frame.
☝️ Draw — straighten your index finger fully, curl the rest.
🤏 Resize — curl your index too, then pinch thumb + index.
🎯 Jittery lines? — raise Stroke Smoothing in Settings.
🐢 Gestures not registering? — lower confidence sliders in Settings.
⌨️ Shortcuts — Ctrl+Z undo · Ctrl+S save · Space pause · F fullscreen · M mirror.
Gesture Guide
